Hey! I'm Brandon

I graduated from the University of California, Riverside with a degree in Psychology. While at UC Riverside I was a research assistant in the Laboratory for Cognition and Action and Visual Cognition Lab. It was here that I was able to see first hand how people can have massively different experiences when presented with the same experiment. I learned that understanding people is a crucial step in creating good design.

After graduating I was fortunate enough to intern at ConnectOurKids working on their mobile app as a UX Designer. I was able to experience what it takes to build a real product for real people.

I am a team player that is always looking to take on new challenges.
