ConnectOurKids

Forever families found faster

Connect Connectourkids is a non-profit organization that aims to connect foster children with a permanent home. Their organization provides free tools to social workers to expeditite their work.

Providing value to social workers

Before designing any screens it was important to define the key features and understand how social workers would get value out of the app. Through discussions with the stakeholder and understanding of the work flow of social workers we were able to layout the most important features.

Understanding social workers needs

The ways in which a social workers need to be able to use this app were separated into 3 main categories: 1.Search for people in the ConnectOurKids database 2.Add and edit contact information for potential family candidates 3.Log past and ongoing activity/engagements with family candidates.

People search

Multiple searching riteria are needed to expand searching capabilities. 5 options of searching are available: • Name • Email • Address • Phone number •Social profile link or URL Frequent searching Social workers need to be able to start a new search quickly. The presence of a clear button is due to the frequent need for users to begin a search with a new information. It is necessary for the social workers entirely clear their previous search with one click. People unfamiliar with the searching features need a quick tutorial. The button for the introduction video will disappear after the tutorial has been watched.
